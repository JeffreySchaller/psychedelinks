Why we exist

For many people, these experiences have mattered.

Psychedelic experiences have played a meaningful role in healing, perspective, and personal growth for countless people. Yet the conversation around them stays misunderstood, stigmatized, and outdated.

Decades of Schedule I classification have not kept pace with the science. Research continues to show real potential to support mental health, including PTSD, depression, and addiction, when approached responsibly.

We believe acceptance is inevitable. Our purpose is to help accelerate it : : through education, credible research, and real human experience.
